About the Olympiad

The National Aviation Olympiad is a pioneering initiative to ignite curiosity and build foundational aviation knowledge among India’s brightest students. Through a multi-stage competition and curated learning resources, we empower future leaders in aerospace, aviation technology, and allied sciences.

Innovation

Cultivating a mindset of experimentation with cutting-edge aviation concepts, including aerodynamics, navigation systems, and sustainability in flight.

National Reach

Connecting schools from metros to emerging cities, ensuring every learner gets access to high-quality aviation education and mentorship opportunities.

Education

Aligning curriculum support with STEM goals for Classes 8–12 while delivering structured pathways to pursue aviation and aerospace careers.

National Olympiad Exam

Designed exclusively for students of Classes 8–12 across India, the Olympiad nurtures critical thinking, flight sciences, and leadership skills through a staged evaluation journey.

  • Begin with foundational science and mathematics concepts before advancing to aviation-specific modules on aerodynamics, propulsion, and navigation.
  • Participate in school-level workshops and webinars hosted by the Expert Committee.
  • Practice with mock tests and timed assessments to build confidence and accuracy.
  • Class 8–9: Basics of flight, physics of motion, introductory meteorology.
  • Class 10: Aircraft systems, aviation history, environmental impact of aviation.
  • Class 11–12: Advanced aerodynamics, aerospace engineering principles, space tech.
  • Downloadable sample papers categorized by class and difficulty.
  • Curated practice questions focusing on real-world aviation scenarios.
  • Interactive quizzes to test speed, accuracy, and conceptual clarity.
